Home

The San Francisco Controller's Office maintains a database of contracts and payments made to Suppliers (vendors, contractors and grantees). The Supplier Contracts include all fully executed, expenditure-only contracts, including City contracts for commodities, professional and general services, and construction from fiscal year 2018 forward. Data: This data set includes both departmental and citywide contracts. One department can have many than one contract, one supplier can have more than on contract, one contract has at least one department and one supplier. Our application is to dynamically display the running balance of each contract.
